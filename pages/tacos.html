Tacos

Carne asada tacos.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 a lb of chopped beef
  • crushed red pepper
  • 1 lime
  • tomatillos 1 can
  • 2 jalapenos
  • canola oil
  • Corn tortillas
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 bunch of chopped cilantro
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Marinate beef in bowl with salt,pepper, crushed red pepper and lime juce for about 30 minutes.
  2. In blender process jalapenos and tomatillos
  3. Gently toast processed mix with oil in a pan for 5 minutes.
  4. Get a skillet or griddle
  5. Cook beef in oil until crispy and brown. As the beef begins to finish cooking heat tortillas on the same griddle as needed.
  6. Assemble the tacos with the cooked beef and tortillas.
  7. Add lemon, avocado and cilantro as preferred.
