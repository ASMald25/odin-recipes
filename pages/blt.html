Bacon Lettuce Tomato

A simple Bacon Lettuce and Tomato sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook bacon in skillet for 10 minutes at med/high heat until crispy. Drain on paper-towel lined plate
  3. Spread Mayo on toasted bread and arrange the rest of the ingredients
  4. Close the sandwhich and enjoy!
