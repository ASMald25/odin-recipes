Fries

Classic homemade french fries.

Ingredients

  • A large rustic potatoe
  • vegetable oil for frying, about 2 cups
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and cut your potatoe into desired fry shape
  2. Soak in water for about 30min
  3. Pat dry
  4. Fry in batches for 5 minutes at around 275 degrees.
  5. Fry in batches till cripsy at around 350 degrees.
  6. Enjoy!
