Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Fried chicken is chicken dipped in batter and then fried. It is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside. It's some of the best America has to offer.

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks and thighs
  • salt
  • frying oil
  • black pepper
  • paprika
  • buttermilk
  • White flour
  • baking powder
  • garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • egg

Steps

  1. Preheat your oil in pan to around 250 degrees
  2. Sprinkle chicken with salt, black pepper and paprika
  3. Let chicken soak in buttermilk for atleast 30 min or up to an entire day
  4. After letting your chicken soak dip it into flour and baking soda
  5. Then mix enough eggs into a bowl of buttermilk to turn it yellow
  6. Rub chicken in bowl until completely covered with buttermilk and egg
  7. Redip into flour mixed with baking soda
  8. Finally fry on both sides until light brown and crispy
  9. Lightly dust with onion powder, garlic powder and paprika
  10. enjoy
