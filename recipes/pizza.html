Cheese Pizza Recipe

Description

Nothing beats a well done Mozzarella pizza. Melted chunks of Mozzarella over a light sauce on a tan crust.

Ingredients

  • Low moisture mozzarella
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • yeast
  • white flour
  • mashed tomatoes
  • black pepper
  • garlic powder
  • eggs
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to atleast 400 before cooking
  2. Start with one cup of flour and half a cup of water. Add enough salt to be lightly noticible.
  3. Add an eggs until the texture is thick but not runny. The dough should be sticky once mixed.
  4. Knead until uniform and form a ball
  5. Add yeast and a little bit of sugar water to help the yeast. It should not cause the taste to be sweet
  6. Mix and cover with a cloth in a bowl. Let rise for atleast 1 hour
  7. While the dough rises. Mix your mashed tomatoes with a few pinches of salt and some dallops of olive oil
  8. The sauce shouldn't be taste too differently from plain tomatoes. Add enough garlic powder to lightly taste it in the sauce.
  9. Once your dough and sauce are ready. Roll out your dough to roughly be 15% bigger than the pizza you want. It will shrink in the oven.
  10. lightly coat your dough in olive oil. Then add your layer of sauce. Don't make it to thick. It should be just enough to not show the dough underneath.
  11. After you sauce, add your chunks of mozzarella. Don't put too much or the pizza will be greasy. just enough that every slice would have some cheese on it.
  12. Cook roughly between 8 to 15min. The crust will be a tan color with some spots when done.
