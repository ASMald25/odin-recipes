French Fries Recipe

Description

French Fries are potatoes that have been sliced into rectangles, salted and then fried. They are a well known favorite all over the world specifically in fast food. They pair well with sauces such as ketchup or ranch.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • frying oil
  • pepper
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven(300C) or oil(300C)
  2. Peel potatoes and submerge in water
  3. After letting your potatoes soak for a while(atleast 15min) chop into rectangles.
  4. Pat your fries dry and then cook them
  5. Fries should be a golden or light brown color
  6. Sprinkle with salt and paprika
